Technology Focus

Doriath Technology is one of the IT companies providing full cycle system integration services in the Telecommunications market - design, development and support of integrated information systems of any complexity.

Having chosen open standards, advanced communication technologies and software corresponding to the most sophisticated corporate requirements as the company's offerings basis, Doriath Technology provides creation and support of reliable failure-safe information infrastructure for Telecom Network Operators of any size.

The company and its engineering and technology teams have established a good reputation of a highly experienced partner in such areas, as:

    • Telecom IT systems audit
    • IT consulting around Billing and Customer Care systems
    • Deployment and management of Billing solutions
    • Serving projects as Project Management, Design Authority and Solution Architecture
    • Development and Consultancy of Service Delivery Platforms
    • Applications integration
    • Custom software and embedded development
    • Maintenance and support of complex software systems

Hi-tech solutions from the world-known vendors - data storage systems, server complexes, enterprise applications, networks and telecommunications are used to achieve the successful results.

Understanding of the customer's business aims and problems, wide expertise and qualified audit, clear problem statement and competent solutions, allowing improving the client's enterprise performance, become more and more valuable in today's world.

Doriath Technology provides custom projects management and organization, through offering IT consulting, design and development of software and embedded systems according to specific customer's requirements.

Through the use of modern object-oriented design and programming tools as well as standard well-known software development methodology the company offers application integration, metadata management, distributed applications implementation, support and maintenance, legacy code and embedded systems migration.
